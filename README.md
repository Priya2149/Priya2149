## 🌟 About Me
Hey there! I’m Priya — a Software Engineer with 2.5+ years of experience building cloud-native, scalable apps using Azure, .NET Core, Node.js, and Docker.

🎓 I’m currently pursuing my M.S. in Computer Science @ Pace University, focusing on AI, NLP, Computer Vision, and DevOps.

💡 I’m not just about code — I’m also passionate about entrepreneurship and product development. I love exploring how technical innovation, user-focused design, and business strategy come together to create products people actually love.

🚀 Right now, I’m working on an AI Governance Dashboard to track LLM usage, cost, latency, and security risks — blending AI safety, cloud engineering, and enterprise needs.

✨ I thrive in Agile teams, enjoy solving tough problems, and believe that every challenge is an opportunity to build something impactful.
